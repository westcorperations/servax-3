What is ServaX

ServaX is an all-in-one ecosystem, allowing you to communicate with your family, friends and loved ones, transact, learn, and do so much more. The only Jack of all trades that masters them all. At ServaX, we realize that it is pretty impossible to seperate communication, financial transactions and learning from our day to day lives. Think about how many times you talk, pay and get paid, and learn in one day. Imagine doing all that in one place with the security that the blockchain offers. That’s ServaX.






Introducing ServaX




ServaX Academy

This is the learning platform of ServaX. This free learning platform allows our users to learn and develop themselves in any area of their financial lives - with certificates on completion. Expert users in various fields will contribute to this platform and earn ServaCoin tokens as rewards.



ServaX Social

This is the interacting and communication arm of ServaX. It is an end-to-end micro blogging site, the source code which will be made public upon completion. Our features include video and audio conferencing. Users can add, follow and react to other users.



ServaX Wallet

With ServaX Wallet, users can check prices, trends and other trading parameters for all tokens of interest on the in-built chart feature. Users can also store their NFTs on this Multi-Chain wallet. Users can also send tokens within the ServaX ecosystem at lightning speed.



ServaX Market

Create, sell and store your NFTs on ServaX. People can find NFTs for each user since each user with an NFT has a direct button on his collection. Payment system powered by the ServaX ecosytem, and is fast and secure.



ServaX Change

ServaXChange is a decentralized exchange, allowing our users interact with their favourite tokens. Our DEX features an intuitive user interface with the lowest possible trading fees.



ServaX Commerce

This arm of ServaX is to enable buying and selling within and outside the ecosystem. Merchants can create Virtual Storefronts at the click of a button and recieve payment for their products in crypto through APIs.
